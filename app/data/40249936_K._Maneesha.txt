Kukkapalli Maneesha (born 29 April 1995) is an Indian badminton player who currently plays women's and mixed doubles. She partners Sanyogita Ghorpade for women's doubles events and previously partnered with J. Meghana, P. V. Sindhu.Doubles Partners For mixed doubles events she partners with Satwiksairaj Rankireddy, and previously, Manu Attri and K.
