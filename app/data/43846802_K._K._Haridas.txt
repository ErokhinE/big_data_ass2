K. K. Haridas (7 September 1965 – 26 August 2018) was an Indian film director in Malayalam cinema. His best known works are Vadhu Doctoranu, Kakkakum Poochakkum Kalyanam and Kinnam Katta Kallan. ==Life== Haridas was born to Kunjukunju and Sarojini in Mylapra village in Pathanamthitta district, Kerala, India. Haridas was married to Anitha and the couple have two children—Haritha and Suryadas. Music composer Kannur Rajan is his brother-in-law.
