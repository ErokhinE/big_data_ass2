K. Malaisamy is an Indian politician from Tamil Nadu. He served as a Member of Parliament in Rajya Sabha, the upper house of the Indian Parliament. Malaisamy was a member of the All India Anna Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam (AIADMK) until 2014.
