K Maragatham (ta:கே.மரகதம்) (born 27 December 1982) is an Indian politician and Member of Parliament elected from Tamil Nadu. She was elected to the Lok Sabha from Kancheepuram constituency as an Anna Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam (AIADMK) candidate in 2014 election.
