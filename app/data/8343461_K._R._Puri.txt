K. R. Puri was Governor of the Reserve Bank of India from 20 August 1975 to 2 May 1977. He was the Chairman and Managing Director of the Life Insurance Corporation of India prior to his appointment as Governor of the RBI. His signature appears on one of the last Indian rupee notes of 1,000 denomination.
